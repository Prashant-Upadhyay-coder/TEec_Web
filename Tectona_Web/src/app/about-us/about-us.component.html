Our Story?

Based in India, Tectonas is a Cyber Security Audit Automation product company.

Based on the paradigm of Cyber Security - Simplified; our moto is to reduce the burden and make cyber security easy and efficient for the IT and admin personnel in various Industries and organizations.

At Tectonas we also understand that an IT networks need would vary from a small to a large organization too. Hence we ensure that our Cyber Security platforms and softwares are flexible and customizable depending on the type and changing needs of an organization.

Why choose us?

Flexible and Customized - All OwnYit products by Tectonas are designed in a way that it can be adapted to the varying needs of an organization.

Problem Solving Approach - At Tectonas, we do not believe in a one fit for all solution. We analyze the problem areas and accordingly suggest options that could solve the problem.

Experienced Team - We have a team of highly trained and skilled ENDEC team who has successfully secured over 200 clients across 8 countries by improving their IT visibility, reduce IT operation costs, mitigate cyber security threats, comply cyber security standards and lot more.

Easily Accessible - We ensure that our products don't need a technical personnel to operate and can be used by Business owners as well as IT Managers who can be given role based access too.

Compatibility - All Tectonas products are compatible with popular Operating systems like Windows and Linux.

Established Distribution System - All our products are made available by their own dedicated channels of exclusive dealers, distributors and delivery partners based across India.
